# Sovereign Stack Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Sovereign Stack exists to serve autonomous communities building systems that prioritize human flourishing over extractive profit. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and productive environment for all contributors.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub repositories, discussions, issues, pull requests, email communications, and any official project channels.

---

## Our Values

The Sovereign Stack is guided by these principles:

**Flow over containment**
- Share knowledge freely
- Enable collaboration, not gatekeeping
- Circulate credit and recognition

**Replication over scaling**
- Support community autonomy
- Encourage forking and adaptation
- Prioritize local resilience over centralized growth

**Sovereignty as verb, not noun**
- Active participation in governance
- Shared responsibility for community health
- Accountability to collective benefit

**Community benefit over private profit**
- Reinvest resources in the commons
- Resist extractive commercialization
- Maintain the asset lock

---

## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

### Be Respectful
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- Assume good intent until proven otherwise
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect boundaries and consent

### Be Collaborative
- Work together toward shared goals
- Offer constructive feedback
- Help newcomers learn and grow
- Share knowledge generously

### Be Accountable
- Take responsibility for your actions and their impact
- Apologize when you make mistakes
- Learn from feedback and grow
- Follow through on commitments

### Be Mission-Aligned
- Contribute in ways that advance community autonomy
- Resist efforts to capture or enclose the commons
- Prioritize collective benefit over individual gain
- Protect the project's integrity and values

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

### Harassment and Discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, religion, or other protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events

### Disrespectful Conduct
- Personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

### Undermining Project Integrity
- Attempting to circumvent licensing terms
- Proposing changes that violate the asset lock
- Promoting extractive commercial exploitation
- Bad-faith participation or sabotage

### Abuse of Power
- Using maintainer/contributor status for personal gain
- Favoring commercial interests over community benefit
- Retaliating against those who report violations
- Creating conflicts of interest

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

**For immediate safety concerns:**
- Contact local authorities if physical safety is at risk

**For community conduct issues:**
- GitHub: https://github.com/vanj900
- Include: Description of incident, date/time, individuals involved, any supporting evidence
- Confidentiality: Reports are handled confidentially

### What Happens Next

1. **Acknowledgment**: We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: Maintainers will review the report and gather information
3. **Decision**: Maintainers will determine appropriate action
4. **Communication**: We will inform the reporter of the outcome (respecting privacy as appropriate)

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity, maintainers may:

**Minor Violations:**
- Private written warning
- Request for public apology
- Temporary participation restrictions

**Moderate Violations:**
- Temporary ban from project spaces (e.g., 30 days)
- Removal of specific contributions
- Public acknowledgment of violation

**Severe Violations:**
- Permanent ban from all project spaces
- Report to relevant authorities (if illegal)
- Public statement about the violation

**Factors considered:**
- Severity and impact of behavior
- Intent and context
- History of past violations
- Willingness to make amends

---

## Appeals

If you disagree with an enforcement decision:

1. **GitHub**: https://github.com/vanj900
2. **Include**: Original incident details, enforcement action taken, your perspective
3. **Timeline**: Appeals reviewed within 14 days

Appeal decisions are final.

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Addressing violations promptly and transparently
- Protecting reporter confidentiality
- Modeling expected behavior
- Stepping down if unable to enforce impartially

Maintainers who violate this Code of Conduct may face removal from their role.
