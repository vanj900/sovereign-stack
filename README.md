## Overview

The Sovereign Stack is a blueprint for building self-governing, resilient communities that operate independently of centralized state and corporate infrastructure. It integrates physical architecture, distributed energy systems, peer-to-peer governance, and regenerative life support into a single forkable civilization design.

This is not a software platform, a housing project, or a political movement. It is all three‚Äîand more‚Äîunified by a core principle: **flow over containment**.

## Core Principles

The Sovereign Stack is built on three architectural constraints (see [CORE.md](CORE.md) for details):

1. **Flow Over Containment** - Resources circulate rather than accumulate
2. **Sovereignty via Forkability** - Communities replicate horizontally, never centralize
3. **Truth by Receipts** - Accountability through cryptographic proof, not authority

**Key characteristics:**
- **Energy flows peer-to-peer** without central grids
- **Governance operates through small cells** (3-7 people) that fork instead of fight
- **Physical infrastructure is mobile yet permanent** (SOV-HAB dwellings)
- **AI advises, humans decide** (no algorithmic authority)
- **Communities replicate, not scale** (growth through forking)
- **All decisions are transparent** (immutable receipts, auditable trust)

## System Architecture

The Sovereign Stack operates as an organism with four integrated layers:

### 1. **The Metabolism: Energy Flow Network**
Peer-to-peer electrical power routing using micro-buffers and AI-mediated reciprocity. No central grid. Each node maintains 2-3 days autonomy.

### 2. **The Nervous System: Sovereign Mesh**
Local communications network (WiFi + LoRa) operating independently of ISPs and the internet. Enables governance, coordination, and data exchange even during infrastructure collapse.

### 3. **The Mind: GhostStack (Civic OS)**
Governance through small autonomous cells. Decisions made via proposals and recorded as immutable receipts. When conflict becomes irreconcilable, communities fork peacefully rather than fracture violently.

### 4. **The Cognitive Layer: Human-AI Symbiosis**
Protects human attention and agency through adaptive interfaces, therapeutic protocols, and AI advisors constrained by human oversight (Node Veto Escalation Protocol).

## Governance Model

### Small Cell Democracy
- **Cell Size:** 3-7 people (prevents hierarchy)
- **Decision Process:** Proposal ‚Üí AI Analysis ‚Üí Deliberation ‚Üí Vote ‚Üí Receipt
- **Transparency:** All governance receipts public within mesh
- **Conflict Resolution:** Communities fork rather than fight

### Forking: Peaceful Separation
When disagreement becomes irreconcilable:
1. **ForkManifest** documents the split
2. **Digital assets** (code, history, data) duplicated to both forks
3. **Physical assets** divided via pre-agreed rules or mediation
4. **Alliances** must be re-ratified by new fork

Both communities continue operating independently. No winners or losers.

## Philosophical Foundation

**‚Üí See [CORE.md](CORE.md) for the complete philosophical and architectural foundation**

This project emerged from a specific context: years navigating institutional systems (courts, care homes, tribunals) that claimed to serve human welfare while systematically failing the humans within them.

The pattern was consistent: **containment over flow**. Energy stored rather than circulated. Information hoarded rather than shared. Power accumulated rather than distributed.

The Sovereign Stack is a direct response. Not reform, but transcendence. Not violence against the old system, but construction of something it cannot contain, co-opt, or collapse.

This is **trauma-informed engineering**. Every subsystem is designed so no single point of failure‚Äîtechnical, political, or cognitive‚Äîcan break the civilization.

**"Revenge through creation."**

The three irreducible axioms in [CORE.md](CORE.md) define what this system **must** be:
1. **Flow Over Containment** - Stability through circulation, not accumulation
2. **Sovereignty via Forkability** - Exit rights over enforced unity
3. **Truth by Receipts** - Proof over authority
