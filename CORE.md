# CORE.md

The Sovereign Stack is not merely a set of technologies; it is a "metaphysical correction" to the logic of modern civilization [1]. The system is built upon three irreducible principles that function as its physics. These cannot be altered without breaking the stack.

## 1. Flow Over Containment
**"Containment breeds stagnation; flow breeds resilience."** [2]

We reject the industrial logic of hoarding resources in static reservoirs—whether energy in grids, capital in banks, or power in bureaucracies [3]. In the Sovereign Stack, stability is achieved through the dynamic circulation of resources (energy, data, trust) rather than their accumulation.
*   **The Mechanism:** Energy is not stored in massive centralized batteries but routed instantly to where it creates value or is converted into other forms (heat, computation, bonds) [4], [5].
*   **The Imperative:** Civilization must metabolize resources like a living organism. If flow stops, the system dies [6].

## 2. Sovereignty via Forkability
**"Everything forks, nothing centralizes."** [7]

The system serves the sovereignty of the small group (the Cell) over the efficiency of the large state. There are "No thrones. No heirs" [8]. To prevent the rot of centralization, the system refuses to scale vertically.
*   **The Mechanism:** The basic unit is the Cell (3–7 nodes). When a group exceeds this limit, it must **fork** (replicate horizontally) rather than grow into a hierarchy [9], [10]. Dissent is resolved not by coercion, but by forking the code, the ledger, and the culture to create a divergent lineage [11], [12].
*   **The Imperative:** Unity is never enforced. If a system cannot be forked, it is a trap.

## 3. Truth by Receipts
**"Receipts replace bureaucracy."** [13]

Truth is not established by human authority or institutional rank, but by an immutable, append-only chain of cryptographic evidence known as the **Integrity Chain** [14].
*   **The Mechanism:** Every action—a vote, a kilowatt of energy transfer, a medical intervention, or a teaching moment—generates a signed **Receipt** [14], [15]. These receipts form the "universal memory" of the civilization [16].
*   **The Imperative:** Accountability must be mathematical and transparent. Power is something you verify, not something you trust [17].

## Why These Axioms Matter

These three principles are not aspirational—they are **architectural constraints**. They define what the Sovereign Stack can and cannot be.

### What Follows from Flow Over Containment

- Energy systems must be **bidirectional** (Energy Coupler)
- Resources must be **liquid** and immediately routable
- Value must **circulate** rather than accumulate
- Hoarding becomes structurally impossible

### What Follows from Sovereignty via Forkability

- No central authority can control the network
- Communities maintain **exit rights** at all times
- Dissent is **productive** rather than suppressed
- Scale happens through **replication**, not hierarchy
- Governance is **composable** and modular

### What Follows from Truth by Receipts

- All actions leave **cryptographic evidence**
- Trust is replaced by **verification**
- History cannot be rewritten (append-only)
- Accountability is **automatic** and mathematical
- Power requires **proof**, not proclamation

## The Stack as Immune System

These axioms work together to create an **immune response** to centralization, extraction, and capture:

**If someone tries to hoard energy:**
→ Flow-based architecture prevents accumulation beyond immediate storage capacity
→ Surplus energy automatically routes to other nodes or converts to other forms

**If someone tries to centralize power:**
→ Forkability allows communities to exit without permission
→ Horizontal replication makes hierarchy structurally impossible
→ Cells remain small (3-7 nodes) by design

**If someone tries to rewrite history or hide actions:**
→ Integrity Chain provides immutable audit trail
→ Receipts are cryptographically signed and verifiable
→ Trust is replaced by proof

## Non-Negotiable Elements

The following cannot be removed or compromised without destroying the stack's integrity:

### 1. Cell Size Limit (3-7 nodes)
The system **must** fork when cells exceed this size. This is not a suggestion—it's a hard constraint that prevents hierarchical capture.

### 2. Bidirectional Energy Flow
Energy **must** be routable in all directions. Static storage is minimized; dynamic circulation is maximized. The Energy Coupler is not optional.

### 3. Cryptographic Receipts
All meaningful actions **must** generate signed receipts on the Integrity Chain. Accountability cannot be negotiable or optional.

### 4. Fork Rights
Communities **must** retain the right to fork code, governance, and ledgers at any time. Exit is always an option.

### 5. Open Source + Asset Lock
The technology **must** remain open-source under copyleft (AGPL-3.0). Commercial licensing **must** reinvest revenue into community benefit (vanj900 Asset Lock).

## Anti-Patterns: What This Is Not

To clarify what the Sovereign Stack rejects:

### Not a Startup
This is not venture-backed, not exit-seeking, not optimizing for acquisition. vanj900 operates under an **Asset Lock**—all assets serve community benefit, not private profit.

### Not a Government
This is not a proposal for reforming existing institutions. Sovereign Stack communities exist **parallel** to states, not as replacements demanding recognition.

### Not a Commune
This is not utopian or escapist. Sovereign Stack communities engage with the existing economy while maintaining sovereignty over their internal systems.

### Not a Blockchain
While the Integrity Chain uses cryptographic verification, this is not "blockchain for everything." The receipts serve **accountability**, not financialization.

### Not Voluntary
Once adopted, these axioms are **enforced by the architecture**. You cannot opt out of flow-based energy, cell size limits, or cryptographic receipts while remaining on the stack.

## How to Break the Stack (What Not to Do)

The following modifications would fundamentally break the Sovereign Stack:

❌ **Allow cells to grow beyond 7 nodes without forking**
→ Enables hierarchy, destroys sovereignty

❌ **Add centralized energy storage without routing**
→ Creates hoarding, breaks flow principle

❌ **Make receipts optional or deletable**
→ Destroys accountability, enables power without proof

❌ **Remove fork rights or add barriers to exit**
→ Creates capture, violates sovereignty

❌ **License code under permissive terms (MIT, Apache)**
→ Allows proprietary enclosure, violates asset lock

❌ **Optimize for scaling vertically instead of replicating horizontally**
→ Recreates the centralization the stack was built to prevent
